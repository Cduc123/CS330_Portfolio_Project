CS-330 Computational Graphics and Visualization Portfolio Reflection

How do I approach designing software?

I approach designing software by first understanding the requirements and then planning how each feature should work before I begin coding. For this project, I focused on creating a 3D scene that was visually organized and functional. I learned how important it is to think about user experience, object placement, lighting, textures, and camera movement during the design stage.

My work on this project helped me build stronger design skills, especially with spatial thinking and scene organization. I followed a step-by-step design process by starting with simple shapes, combining them into larger objects, and then improving the scene through textures and lighting. These same tactics can be applied in future work when designing websites, applications, or games by planning first and building in stages.

How do I approach developing programs?

I approach developing programs by breaking large tasks into smaller parts and testing often. While working on my 3D scene, I used debugging, trial and error, and iteration to improve each milestone. I first focused on shapes and transformations, then textures, lighting, and camera controls.

Iteration was one of the most important parts of my development process because each milestone helped me refine the previous work. My coding approach has evolved by becoming more organized, patient, and focused on solving one issue at a time until the final project was completed successfully.

How can computer science help me in reaching my goals?

Computer science helps me reach my goals by giving me technical and problem-solving skills that can be applied in many careers. Computational graphics and visualizations gave me experience with OpenGL, 3D rendering, transformations, textures, and lighting. These skills can support my future educational pathway in software development and graphics programming. Professionally, they can help me in fields such as game development, simulations, UI design, and other technology careers that require creativity and technical knowledge.
